type [id] [content] [press_enter_after=0|1]: To type content into a field with a specific ID. By default, the "Enter" key is pressed after typing unless `press_enter_after` is set to 0. E.g., `type [15] [Carnegie Mellon University] [1]`. If you can't find what you're looking for on your first attempt, consider refining your search keywords by breaking them down or trying related terms. To search for a GitLab user: `type [2556] [user] [1]`. To search for a project: `type [2556] [project name] [1]`. For example, `type [2556] [keycloak/keycloak] [1]`. Combining a search for an author and a project will failed. Identify what you're searching for (user, project, issue, etc.) and search for only that type of information.
